MP Safa Al Hashim speaks about updates regarding family law

  • Home
  • Uncategorized
  • MP Safa Al Hashim speaks about updates regarding family law
  • Uncategorized
  • Brandon Watson
  • February 17, 2020February 17, 2020
Head of the Parliamentary Committee for Family and Women, Safaa Al-Hashim, stated that the committee is working to finish the report on domestic abuse legislation before the parliament resumes. MP Al-Hashim said in a comment that the committee will continue studying law articles, and one of the crucial high points of the law is the law asserting the constitutional principles referred to in Article 9 of the Constitution which makes the family the cornerstone of society. “As regards its implementation, we decided the body responsible for that is the supreme council for a family due to its specialization, and being the administrative pillar of the protection centers in charge of receiving, following up, listening to and sheltering victims”, she noted. She asserted the law retains the family’s integrity and solidifies its ties and protects motherhood and infancy. The security stipulated in family law is the key issue that is opposed to domestic violence. In addition, MP Al-Hashim concluded that the law covers an article establishing a coordinating committee containing respective government bodies and civil society and that the committee’s role has already been recognized and created.
